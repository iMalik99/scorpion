Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to our most common guest inquiries.

What are the check-in and check-out times?

Check-in is from 2:00 PM, and check-out is before 12:00 PM (noon).

Is parking available?

Yes, we offer complimentary self-parking and valet parking for all our registered guests.

Do you accept card payments?

Absolutely. We accept all major credit and debit cards, including Visa, MasterCard, American Express, and Verve. Mobile payments are also welcome.

Can I bring my pet?

Unfortunately, while we love animals, Scorpion Hotel maintains a strict no-pet policy to ensure the comfort and safety of all our guests.

What is your cancellation policy?

Cancellations made 48 hours prior to the standard check-in time (2:00 PM) are free of charge. Cancellations within 48 hours or no-shows may incur a one-night room charge.
